About Teleroutex

We connect communities and enterprises through intelligent, reliable wholesale telecom infrastructure built on a decade of real-world experience.

Our Company
Creating Connections That Matter

Teleroutex is a communications platform company positioned at the centre of the global telecom ecosystem. We bridge the gap between wholesale telecommunications infrastructure and the real-world needs of carriers, MVNOs, aggregators, and enterprises.

Our solutions span global voice termination, enterprise SMS delivery, ATX revenue generation, and fraud intelligence each built to operate at carrier scale with the responsiveness of a boutique partner.

We enable every person, application, and connected device to communicate simply and securely, wherever they are in the world.
