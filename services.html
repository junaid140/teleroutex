Our Services

Three specialised wholesale telecom solutions designed to maximise your revenue, protect your network, and ensure every message and call reaches its destination.

Service 01
Voice Fraud Prevention

Teleroutex operates a next-generation fraud prevention system that proactively shields your network from threats on international voice traffic. Our continuously updated threat intelligence database covers virtually every verified fraudulent number pattern in circulation today.

Advanced AI Threat Intelligence

Our AI engine identifies and flags fraudulent activity before it reaches your network giving you the chance to intercept threats and in many cases turn them into a monetisation opportunity rather than a loss.

Machine learning algorithms update continuously in near real time, securing your infrastructure from both known attack types and newly emerging threats. Human expertise and AI processing work in combination so nothing gets through.

What We Block

🛡 IRSF Attacks

International Revenue Share Fraud identified and terminated before minutes are billed to your account.

📞 Wangiri Fraud

One-ring-and-cut callbacks detected and blocked at origination level instantly without human intervention.

🔄 SIM-Box Bypass

Grey route and SIM-box traffic flagged using behavioural pattern analysis running in real time around the clock.

⚠ Emerging Threats

New attack vectors identified through machine learning and added to the block list within hours of first detection.

Service 02
ATX Termination

A complete ecosystem for generating, routing, and securing premium rate voice traffic with full transparency at every stage.

👥 What Our Partners Provide

  • ✓  A steady high-quality origination traffic stream
  • ✓  Volume commitment the more traffic the better the returns
  • ✓  Clean verified origination sources from agreed regions

🌟 What Teleroutex Creates

  • ✓  New inbound traffic through targeted digital marketing
  • ✓  Banners and ads placed on sites across Africa the Middle East and Asia
  • ✓  Creatives designed for chat services gaming platforms and language apps

The ATX Process Step by Step

01

Traffic Collection

Our partner gathers inbound call volume from verified global originations on specific destination numbers.

02

Traffic Handover

Collected traffic is forwarded to Teleroutex at the agreed rate. We are placed first in routing due to competitive pricing.

03

Marketing Activation

Our marketing team launches banners and digital ads with destination numbers embedded, driving new inbound call volume.

04

Click to Call

End users interact with our ads and click through, directly dialling the destination numbers behind the banners.

05

Call Verification

Every inbound call is verified by our system. Unrecognised calls are returned with Error Code 503 with no exceptions.

06

Live Connection

Verified calls are connected to the live operator, IVR, or destination service completing the full ATX cycle cleanly.

🔒

Secure

All unrecognised calls are rejected with Error 503. Your destination numbers are protected at all times without exception.

📈

Normal Traffic Unaffected

Our call verification process does not interrupt or delay regular traffic flows. Volume and quality are maintained throughout.

🚫

Zero Hijack

Only traffic generated through our verified marketing channels is accepted. No third-party hijacking is possible.
